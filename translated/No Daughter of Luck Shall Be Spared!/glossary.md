## Reference Notes

The following reference material was preserved from the original glossary notes:

## Cultivation Realms ##
  
  1. Qi Condensation (凝气)
  2. Foundation Establishment (筑基)
  3. Golden Core (金丹)
  4. Nascent Soul (元婴)
  5. Soul Transformation (化神)
  6. Body Integration (合体)
  7. Void Refining (炼虚)
  8. Grand Ascension (大乘) — highest mortal realm

  Each realm has Early, Middle, and Late stages (初期, 中期, 後期):
  1. Human Immortal (人仙)
  2. Earth Immortal (地仙)
  3. Profound Immortal (玄仙)
  4. Heavenly Immortal (天仙)
  5. True Immortal (真仙)
  6. Golden Immortal (金仙)
  7. Taiyi True Immortal (太乙真仙)
  8. Taiyi Golden Immortal (太乙金仙)
  9. Grand Luo Golden Immortal (大羅金仙) — Title: Immortal King (仙王)
  10. Primordial Grand Luo Golden Immortal (混元大羅金仙) — Title: Immortal Emperor (仙帝)
